Overview

Bhalorant is a game I built for fun using Unity. It's like a simpler version of Valorant, with cool 2D graphics and sounds. I'm still working on it and adding more stuff, but it's a great way to show what I learned about making games with Unity.

Additional Details

I switched from Python and Pygame to Unity for my game development journey. Unity's tutorials and documentation helped me get started. While playing Valorant with friends, I noticed the game was getting bigger with updates. So, I decided to make a 2D version called Bhalorant using Unity and Photon Networking. Creating Bhalorant has been fun, especially because I can play it with friends. I'm still working on it, adding new stuff whenever I can. It's a project I want to keep going for as long as possible.

Key Features

  • Online-Multiplayer: Play with friends in real-time multiplayer matches, connecting players from different locations for thrilling gaming sessions.
  • 2D Shooting: Engage in fast-paced shooting action with intuitive controls and dynamic gameplay mechanics, offering an immersive experience reminiscent of classic shooters.
  • Camera Zoom: Experience strategic depth with adjustable camera zoom, allowing players to adapt their view to different scenarios and optimize their gameplay tactics.
  • Multiple Maps: Explore diverse environments and engage in battles across a variety of maps, each offering unique challenges and opportunities for tactical gameplay.
  • Particles and Sound Effects: Immerse yourself in the action with vibrant particle effects and immersive sound effects, enhancing the intensity and excitement of every encounter.
