  • Project Year: 2019

Overview

MrMaze is a captivating C-based maze game challenging players to escape each level with minimal steps. With 7-8 progressively difficult levels, unlocking sequentially, the game immerses players in navigating intricate 2D mazes, requiring strategy and problem-solving skills to reach the exit.

Additional Details

Following OddEven, my passion for game development spurred me to explore new ideas. Inspired by the Maze Runner movie, I embarked on creating MrMaze, leveraging the same technical skills and knowledge as OddEven. Despite time constraints before college, I dedicated myself to crafting this maze adventure, driven by my love for game development and immersive experiences.

Key Features

  • Simple Design: MrMaze boasts a straightforward design for easy accessibility and enjoyment.
  • Hand-Crafted Maze Levels: Each level features intricately designed hand-crafted mazes, offering unique challenges and surprises.
  • Steps Counter: Keep track of your progress with a steps counter, encouraging players to optimize their routes and escape the maze efficiently.
